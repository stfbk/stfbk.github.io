---
project: DAOFidelity
funding: Next Generation EU (InnovAction)

goals: >
  This project aims to support CherryChain in developing and evolving the DAO Fidelity platform toward an advanced, intelligent security model, integrating Artificial Intelligence technologies and the OpenTelemetry (OTel) observability framework. The initiative aims to overcome the limitations of current approaches based on passive observability and static rules by implementing a solution that proactively anticipates, detects, and responds to cybersecurity threats in distributed and cloud-native environments.
