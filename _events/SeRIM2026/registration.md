<p>Registration is mandatory and the access to the workshop will be granted to registered people only.</p>

<p>As the workshop is co-located with the <a href="https://eurosp2026.ieee-security.org/">11th IEEE European Symposium on Security and Privacy</a> (EuroS&P 2026), please refer to the <a href="https://eurosp2026.ieee-security.org/registration.html">official registration page</a> on the website of the main conference. Please note that registration to the main conference does <b>not</b> grant access to co-located workshops. Moreover, as participation to workshops require a separate registration for each day, please ensure to register to the post-conference workshop day (July 10, 2026).</p>

<p>Please note that at least one author per accepted paper must register <b>before May 31, 2026</b>. The main conference and the co-located workshops are physical events and authors are expected to present their work <b>in person</b>.</p>
